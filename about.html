Kelham Island


This app was made with the purpose of displaying the history and information contained within the Kelham Island musuem. The format of AR is a perfect way to display the exhibit you are looking at as well as the information, being able to show these at the same time for viewing comfort. The app over all enhances the experience for the musuem participant, creating a more positive experience and customer reputation.

Projects like these are perfect and amazing ways to display information of all kinds, many companies will benefit from similar web apps. Companies will benefit from these AR apps, creating a higher regarded repuation and experience.
